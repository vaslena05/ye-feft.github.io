Mandatory Payments

Learn what taxes individual entrepreneurs (IE) and limited liability companies (LLC) are required to pay, how to properly file reports, and submit tax declarations.

Business Registration

What documents are needed to open an IE or LLC, how to choose the type of ownership, and register with tax authorities.

Legal Foundations
of Entrepreneurship

The main laws regulating business activities in Kazakhstan (such as the Civil Code, Tax Code, and the Law on Entrepreneurial Activity).

Consumer Rights Protection and Contracts

How to properly conclude contracts, what rights consumers and entrepreneurs have, and how to act in case of disputes.
